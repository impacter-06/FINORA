
# FINORAA

FINORAA is a financial literacy and smart money guidance platform designed to help students and common users understand personal finance in a simple and practical way.

This platform focuses on improving awareness about budgeting, saving habits, digital banking usage, beginner-friendly investment understanding, and daily money management.

---

## Project Overview

Many students and first-time earners struggle with handling expenses, saving money consistently, understanding online banking tools, and making smart financial decisions.

FINORAA was created as a user-friendly solution to make financial learning easy, accessible, and practical through simple guidance and informative features.

---

## Key Features

- Budget and expense awareness
- Smart saving guidance
- Beginner investment understanding
- Online banking assistance
- Financial habit improvement
- Easy user-friendly interface
